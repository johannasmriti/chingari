Journey Of Strength

Krishna Bansal (five years & five months old) suffers from Developmental Delay. His father abandoned the family when Krishna's mother was eight months pregnant. He lives with his grandmother, who, despite suffering from cancer, cares for him and takes him regularly to theChingari Rehabilitation Centre.

Initially, it was a challenge for Krishna to even sit in the squatting position, but after receiving the physiotherapy sessions, he has made great improvements. This is particularly beneficial for him while using the toilet. He also had balancing issues and used to fall down while running or walking, but now his balancing has improved and he is able to run and walk properly.

Krishna has also become a more cheerful person as his grandmother observes. His socialization skills have improved after attending the special education classes. Earlier, he would cry a lot, throw things and spit in the class, but now he is very well behaved and is in control of his actions to an extent. He also enjoys other children's company.
