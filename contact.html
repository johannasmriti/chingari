Lets's fight toxic terror, Together!

Till today, 1004 congenitally disabled children born in the families affected by Bhopal Gas Tragedy and subsequent water contamination, have been registered with Chingari Trust. However, due to insufficient resources, we are only able to support 300 children who receive Physiotherapy, Speech Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Special Education, Mid-Day Meal, Pick and Drop facility at our Rehabilitation Centre, free of cost.

Like you would know, treating birth defects and handicaps is an expensive affair and such children need to be given special medical facilities on a continuous basis.

It is registered as Chingari Trust (Registration No. 1874/05 New Delhi / PAN AAATC7309L). Donations to Chingari Trust are exempt from 50% tax under 80G of the Income Tax Act.
